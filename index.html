About Me

Director of Data Analysis and Research at SLOCAT Partnership

I am a skilled researcher working at the intersection of sustainable transport, climate action and evidence-driven advocacy. Over the years, I have supported governments, the international transport community and development partners in advancing sustainable, low-carbon transport.

Currently, I lead the research work for the SLOCAT Partnership, developing methodologies, strategies and tools in support of knowledge products and advocacy. My expertise spans data analysis, policy analysis, climate finance and research in the context of global processes on climate change and sustainable development, with a focus on the Global South. I have been based in Seoul (Republic of Korea) since 2012.
